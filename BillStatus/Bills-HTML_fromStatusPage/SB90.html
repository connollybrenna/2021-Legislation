AN ACT adopting omnibus legislation on redistricting.

COMMITTEE: Election Law and Municipal Affairs

ANALYSIS

 

This bill adopts legislation relative to:

 

I.  Resources used by legislative committees in connection with redistricting.

 

II.  Maps created or considered by legislative committees tasked with redistricting.

 

III.  Requiring that any legislative committee meetings on redistricting be open to the public.

2  Legislation Enacted.  The general court hereby enacts the following legislation:

 

PART I

Relative to resources used by legislative committees in connection with redistricting

1  New Section; Redistricting Resources Used for Redistricting.  Amend RSA 14 by inserting after 14-c the following new section:

14:14-d  Redistricting; Resources Used for Redistricting.  Any resources used to assist any legislative committee appointed to consider redistricting after the decennial census that have been paid for with state funds shall be made available to both the majority and minority political parties of both the house of representatives and senate.

2  Effective Date.  Part I of this act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.

 

PART II

Relative to maps created or considered by legislative committees tasked with redistricting.

1  New Section; Redistricting; Maps Created or Considered During Redistricting Process.  Amend RSA 14 by inserting after section 14-c the following new section:

14:14-d  Redistricting; Maps Created or Considered During Redistricting Process.  Any legislative committee appointed to consider redistricting after the decennial census that creates or considers any map of proposed legislative districts shall make such created or considered maps available to the public on a public Internet website.

2  Effective Date.  Part II of this act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.

 

PART III.

Requiring that any legislative committee meetings on redistricting be open to the public.

1  New Section; Redistricting; Public Access to Legislative Meetings.  Amend RSA 14 by inserting after section 14-c the following new section:

14:14-d  Redistricting; Public Access to Legislative Meetings.  Any meeting of any legislative committee appointed to consider redistricting after the decennial census shall be open to the public and made available electronically via live stream.  The public shall be given the opportunity to testify and participate electronically at each meeting held by any legislative committee appointed to consider redistricting.

2  Effective Date.  Part III of this act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
